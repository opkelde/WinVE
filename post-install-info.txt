WinVE Desktop - Installation Complete!

CONGRATULATIONS! WinVE has been successfully installed.

WHAT'S NEXT:

1. LAUNCH WinVE:
   • WinVE should start automatically after installation
   • If not, launch it from Desktop or Start Menu
   • Look for the WinVE icon in your system tray (bottom right)

2. SYSTEM CONFIGURATION & HA INTEGRATION:
   • WinVE runs as a native ESPHome voice satellite.
   • By default, Home Assistant will automatically discover it on your network!
   • Look for the notification or go to Settings → Devices & Services in HA to configure the WinVE device.
   • Right-click the system tray icon → Settings to adjust microphone, output devices, and wake word thresholds.

3. VOICE ACTIVATION:
   • Default hotkey: Ctrl+Shift+H
   • Or use wake words (enabled by default)
   • Models are stored locally - no internet required

4. TROUBLESHOOTING:
   • If wake words don't work: Check microphone selection in Settings.
   • Check logs or restart the app if you make setting changes.

ENJOY YOUR VOICE ASSISTANT!

WinVE is now running in your system tray and ready to use.
